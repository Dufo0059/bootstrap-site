Say La Vie is a brand that is all about self expression. Clothing is a unique way to express emotions and feelings, all clothing created by the Say La Vie team represents our lifes in the quiet town of Ottawa and our hunger to reach to the world spreading good vibes and a positive message. Say La Vie comes from our french routes and the expression that life aint always good and it can sometimes seems like its un fair but You cant change the past only the future so when some fucked up shit happens you gotta just say (la vie) and move on. this is the start of a journey of a life time and we are ready for the ride. Now the only thing left to ask is , Are you going to join us ?
